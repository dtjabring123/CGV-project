In Loving Memory


For years before your birth, your grandfather was a zealous archeologist, travelling strange and dangerous landscapes to collect mystical and mysterious objects.

Before he could share his knowledge and experience with you, he died searching for the final items to complete his collection of arcane artifacts: 3 gems said to have magical properties.

It is your duty to embark on a journey to find these gems and honour your grandfather!

You will have to travel through a wild and mysterious jungle, the frosty tundra, and a desolate pyramid.

Explore with caution, traveller, for the strange environments may not be the only challenges you face...
